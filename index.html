How to Use

Complete guide from first setup to daily attendance with Firebase.

1

Host scanner.html

Upload scanner.html to any static host: GitHub Pages, Netlify, or your own server. Note down the URL.

2

Configure Setup

Go to the Setup tab. Enter your company name, office location, and the scanner URL from step 1. Click Save Settings.

3

Add Employees

Go to the Employees tab. Add staff one by one, or import from Excel (columns: Name, Department).

4

Generate and Print QR

Go to Generate QR, click Generate, then Download PNG. Print it in A4 and place it at your office entrance. The QR never expires.

📱

Daily Use (Employees)

Employees open their phone camera, scan the QR, and tap the link. They pick their name and tap Mark Attendance. Done — no app needed.

📊

Viewing & Exporting

Go to Attendance Log to see all records. Filter by name, date, or status. Click Export to Excel to download a .xlsx file.

❓

Common Questions

Do I regenerate the QR every day?
No — once generated the same QR works forever.

Where is data stored?
In Firebase Firestore — accessible from anywhere.

Can an employee mark twice?
No — duplicates on the same day are blocked.
